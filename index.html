Quote of the moment:
Art is everywhere around you. Just let your heart sink in the beauty of the world. From the way the smell of coffee on a cold morning makes you feel a little bit more ready to start your day, the fact that the girl next door always carries a book with her, anywhere she goes, to the way your heart beats faster when you lock eyes with your lover... There is no ending point to art... Because everything is art.

CalligraphArt is about a girl trying to let the art she feels her soul being filled with escape into the world. Once upon a time, she truly felt the need to transform her hand into a living quill.... But the beginning was rough, staining her hands with colored ink, practicing each letter, only to end up throwing away papers full of mistakes. She thought about giving up, but after a time spent apart from her tools, her heart felt the urge to give life to its vibration. Her emotions, sometimes hard to control, became her living art.
And so she ended up here: with a laptop on her knees, transforming her large collection of drawings and lettering, of which she is now proud, into a virtual diary ready to be seen by the world.
